# Israeli Childcare Timeline: Birth to Age 12

## Birth (Day 1)

- **Register birth** at hospital or population registry (Misrad HaPnim)
- **Child allowance** starts automatically after birth registration
- **Child savings plan** (chisachon) -- choose bank or provident fund within 6 months (defaults to bank if no choice)

## Ages 0-3: Daycare Phase

### Immediately after birth
- Check daycare subsidy eligibility (Ministry of Labor)
- If eligible, apply early (4-8 week processing time)
- Verify daycare is on the Ministry's recognized facility list

### Key deadlines
- Daycare subsidy applications: rolling, but apply before placement
- Space in recognized daycares fills up quickly in central cities

## Age 3: Preschool Begins

### December (year before enrollment)
- Municipality sends registration information

### February-March
- **Gan registration window** -- register at municipal Agaf Chinuch
- Choose religious stream: Mamlachti, Dati, Torani, Charedi
- Pay annual fee: 300-700 NIS

### September
- Gan year begins
- Register for tza'haronim (afternoon program) if needed -- separate registration

## Ages 3-5: Preschool Years

| Year | Level | Compulsory |
|------|-------|------------|
| Age 3 | Trom-Trom Chova | No (free) |
| Age 4 | Trom-Chova | No (free) |
| Age 5 | Gan Chova | Yes |

## Age 6: Elementary School

### January-February (year before enrollment)
- School registration via municipality
- Same religious stream options as gan

### Special education
- If concerns arise, school begins intervention process
- Va'ada referral possible from age 3 onward

## Ages 6-12: Elementary Years

### Ongoing
- Tza'haronim available through age 12
- Gifted program screening typically at ages 7-8 (grades 2-3)
- Annual review of special education support hours

## Cross-Cutting: Child Allowance (Throughout)

| Event | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Birth | Automatic enrollment |
| Within 6 months | Choose savings plan option |
| Monthly | Payment on the 20th |
| Age 18 | Child can access savings plan funds |

## Monthly Amounts (2026)

- 1st child: 173 NIS
- 2nd-4th child: 219 NIS each
- 5th+ child: 173 NIS each
- Savings: 58 NIS/month (or 116 NIS with parental top-up)

## Important Annual Dates

| Month | Event |
|-------|-------|
| January | Child allowance amounts updated (CPI adjustment) |
| February-March | Gan/school registration window |
| April-May | Va'ada meetings for next school year |
| September | School year begins |
| October | Tza'haronim registration typically closes |
