# Psychometric Entrance Test (PET) Preparation Guide

This reference provides detailed preparation strategies, recommended materials, practice test sources, and score improvement tips for the Israeli Psychometric Entrance Test (Bchina Psichometrit, בחינה פסיכומטרית), also known as the PET.

## Test Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Full name | Psychometric Entrance Test (PET) |
| Hebrew name | הבחינה הפסיכומטרית |
| Administered by | NITE (National Institute for Testing and Evaluation, המרכז הארצי לבחינות ולהערכה) |
| Score range | 200-800 |
| National average | Approximately 530 |
| Test dates | 4 per year (April, July, September, December) |
| Duration | Approximately 2 hours 45 minutes |
| Sections | 8 total (some pilot, not scored) |

## Test Structure by Section

### Verbal Reasoning (Chashiva Milulit, חשיבה מילולית) - 40% of Total Score

| Question Type | Hebrew | Count (approx.) | Time per Section |
|--------------|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Analogies (Analogiot) | אנלוגיות | 6-8 | 20 minutes |
| Sentence Completion (Hashlama) | השלמה | 6-8 | Included above |
| Reading Comprehension (Havana) | הבנת הנקרא | 5-7 | Included above |
| Logic (Higayon) | היגיון | 4-6 | Included above |

**Key strategies:**
- Build vocabulary systematically: study 15-20 new words daily
- Practice identifying relationship types in analogies (cause/effect, part/whole, synonym/antonym)
- For reading comprehension, read the questions first, then the passage
- Logic questions test inference, not opinion. Focus on what MUST be true based on the given information

### Quantitative Reasoning (Chashiva Kamutit, חשיבה כמותית) - 40% of Total Score

| Topic | Hebrew | Frequency | Difficulty |
|-------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Algebra (Algebra) | אלגברה | High | Medium-High |
| Geometry (Geometriya) | גיאומטריה | High | Medium-High |
| Data Analysis (Nitu'ach Netunim) | ניתוח נתונים | Medium | Medium |
| Percentages (Achuzim) | אחוזים | High | Low-Medium |
| Probability (Histabru't) | הסתברות | Low-Medium | Medium-High |
| Word Problems (Be'ayot Miluliyot) | בעיות מילוליות | High | Medium |
| Number Theory | תורת המספרים | Low | Medium-High |

**Key strategies:**
- Master fundamentals first: fractions, percentages, ratios, basic algebra
- Learn shortcut methods for common calculations
- Practice graph and table reading; data interpretation questions are high-frequency
- Draw diagrams for geometry problems; do not try to solve them mentally
- Work through problems in order of difficulty (easy first), then return to harder ones

### English Section (Anglit, אנגלית) - 20% of Total Score

| Question Type | Count (approx.) | Time |
|--------------|-----------------|------|
| Vocabulary in context | 6-8 | 20 minutes |
| Sentence Completion | 6-8 | Included above |
| Reading Comprehension | 5-7 | Included above |

**Key strategies:**
- Read English-language news articles daily (The Economist, BBC, etc.)
- Study academic vocabulary lists (AWL - Academic Word List)
- Practice identifying tone and purpose in passages
- For vocabulary questions, use context clues and elimination

## Preparation Timeline

### 6-Month Plan (Recommended)

| Month | Focus | Activities |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| Month 1 | Diagnostic + Fundamentals | Take one full practice test. Identify weakest areas. Begin reviewing basic math and vocabulary. |
| Month 2 | Core Skills | Study algebra, geometry, and data analysis systematically. Begin verbal reasoning drills. |
| Month 3 | Deep Practice | Work through section-specific practice sets. Focus on weakest section. Start English vocabulary building. |
| Month 4 | Full Tests + Review | Take a full practice test every week. Analyze mistakes in detail. Focus on time management. |
| Month 5 | Targeted Improvement | Drill weak question types. Practice under timed conditions. Take 2 full tests this month. |
| Month 6 | Final Preparation | Take 2-3 full tests under exam conditions. Light review only in final week. Manage stress. |

### 3-Month Accelerated Plan

| Month | Focus | Activities |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| Month 1 | Diagnostic + Intensive Review | Take diagnostic. Study weakest areas intensively. Practice daily. |
| Month 2 | Full Tests + Targeted Practice | Weekly full test. Daily section practice. Focus on speed and accuracy. |
| Month 3 | Refinement + Exam Readiness | 2 full tests per week. Fine-tune strategies. Rest in final 3 days. |

## Score Improvement Tips

### General Strategies

| Strategy | Expected Improvement | Time Investment |
|----------|---------------------|----------------|
| Take a formal preparation course | 50-100 points average | 3-5 months, 6-10 hours/week |
| Self-study with official materials | 30-70 points average | 3-6 months, 5-8 hours/week |
| Private tutoring (focused) | 40-80 points average | 2-4 months, 3-5 hours/week |
| Practice tests only (no study) | 10-30 points average | 1-2 months |

### By Score Range

| Current Score | Target | Strategy |
|--------------|--------|----------|
| Below 500 | 550-600 | Focus on fundamentals. Spend 60% of time on weakest section. |
| 500-600 | 650-700 | Balance all three sections. Practice time management. Weekly full tests. |
| 600-700 | 700-750 | Fine-tune strategies. Focus on hard questions. Minimize careless errors. |
| 700+ | 750-800 | Practice speed. Target zero mistakes on easy/medium questions. Master complex question types. |

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Not reading questions carefully | Losing 20-40 points | Underline key words in each question |
| Spending too long on hard questions | Missing easier points | Skip and return; each question is worth the same |
| Not guessing on unknown answers | Losing 10-20 points | No penalty for wrong answers; always guess |
| Neglecting English section | Losing 15-30 points | English is 20% of score; study vocabulary daily |
| Test anxiety | Variable, 20-50 points | Practice under timed conditions; use relaxation techniques |
| Studying only strong sections | Diminishing returns | Improvement in weak sections yields more points |

## Test Day Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| Sleep | Get 7-8 hours the night before |
| Arrival | Arrive 30 minutes early with Teudat Zehut |
| Materials | Bring pencils (No. 2/HB), eraser, sharpener. Calculators are NOT allowed |
| Pacing | Allocate time per section; do not spend more than 2 minutes per question |
| Guessing | Always answer every question; no penalty for guessing |
| Breaks | Short breaks between sections; use them to rest your eyes and stretch |
| Focus | If you finish a section early, review your answers |

## Retaking the PET

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Frequency | Can take every test date (4 per year) |
| Best score policy | Universities accept the highest score from any attempt |
| Waiting period | None required between attempts |
| Score validity | Check with target university; typically valid for several years |
| Typical improvement | 30-70 points on second attempt with additional preparation |
| Diminishing returns | Third and subsequent attempts show smaller gains on average |
| Maximum attempts | Unlimited |
