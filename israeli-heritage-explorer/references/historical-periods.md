# Israeli Historical Periods -- Search Guide

## Ottoman Period (1517-1917)

### Key Search Terms
- English: `Palestine`, `Ottoman`, `Holy Land`, `Yishuv`, `Jaffa`, `Jerusalem`
- Hebrew: `ארץ ישראל`, `ירושלים`, `יפו`, `היישוב הישן`

### Notable Events & Topics
- First Aliyah (1882-1903): agricultural settlements, Petah Tikva, Rishon LeZion
- Second Aliyah (1904-1914): kibbutz movement, Hebrew revival, Tel Aviv founding (1909)
- WWI impact on Palestine: locust plague, deportations, British conquest (1917)

### Collection Tips
- Land ownership records (tabu/kushan documents)
- Sharia court records
- Photographs by American Colony photographers
- Maps by PEF (Palestine Exploration Fund)

## British Mandate (1917-1948)

### Key Search Terms
- English: `Palestine`, `Mandate`, `Balfour`, `White Paper`, `Haganah`, `Irgun`
- Hebrew: `מנדט`, `פלשתינה`, `הכרזת בלפור`, `הספר הלבן`, `הגנה`

### Notable Events & Topics
- Balfour Declaration (1917) and League of Nations Mandate (1920)
- Third Aliyah (1919-1923) and Fourth Aliyah (1924-1929)
- Fifth Aliyah (1929-1939): German Jewish immigration
- 1936-1939 Arab Revolt
- WWII and Holocaust rescue efforts
- 1947 UN Partition Plan, 1948 British withdrawal

### Collection Tips
- Administrative documents in English (Mandate government records)
- Immigration certificates and ship manifests
- Underground press (Hebrew, Yiddish)
- Survey of Palestine maps (detailed topographic surveys)
- Herbert Samuel and other High Commissioner papers

## Early Statehood (1948-1967)

### Key Search Terms
- English: `Israel`, `independence`, `War of Independence`, `mass immigration`, `development towns`
- Hebrew: `עצמאות`, `מלחמת השחרור`, `עלייה המונית`, `מעברות`, `עיירות פיתוח`

### Notable Events & Topics
- Declaration of Independence (May 14, 1948)
- 1948 War and armistice agreements
- Mass immigration: Operation Magic Carpet (Yemen), Operation Ezra and Nehemiah (Iraq)
- Ma'abarot (transit camps) and development towns
- Sinai Campaign (1956)
- Eichmann Trial (1961)
- Water carrier (Movil HaMayim HaArtzi) completed 1964

### Collection Tips
- Government publications and Knesset protocols
- IDF archives (partial access)
- Immigrant absorption documentation
- Photographs by Government Press Office (GPO)
- Radio broadcasts (Kol Yisrael)

## Modern Period (1967-present)

### Key Search Terms
- English: `Six-Day War`, `Yom Kippur`, `peace process`, `Oslo`, `intifada`, `settlements`
- Hebrew: `מלחמת ששת הימים`, `מלחמת יום הכיפורים`, `הסכמי אוסלו`, `התנחלויות`

### Notable Events & Topics
- Six-Day War (1967) and territorial changes
- Yom Kippur War (1973)
- Camp David Accords (1978) and peace with Egypt
- First Lebanon War (1982)
- First Intifada (1987-1993)
- Oslo Accords (1993)
- Second Intifada (2000-2005)
- Gaza disengagement (2005)
- Contemporary oral history projects

### Collection Tips
- Oral history recordings (National Sound Archive)
- Contemporary photography collections
- Academic research publications
- Cultural documentation projects
- Newspaper digitization projects (historical Hebrew press)
